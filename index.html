🚇 Weekend Transport Tips

  • Public transport runs 24/7 on weekends (Friday night to Sunday night)
  • Zone A covers city center, Zone B extends to city limits
  • Most attractions are in Zone A&B
  • Buy tickets from machines or BVG app
  • Always validate paper tickets before use
