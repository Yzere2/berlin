Berlin Museums Guide

World-class collections and unique exhibitions

Museum Island (Museumsinsel)

€24 Day Ticket Book Online

Highlights: Pergamon Altar, Nefertiti Bust, Ishtar Gate, Byzantine Art

Details: UNESCO World Heritage Site with 5 world-class museums

Hours: Tue-Sun 10:00-18:00, Thu until 20:00, Closed Mondays

Individual prices: €12-14 per museum

Pergamon Museum

€14 Online Essential

Highlights: Ishtar Gate, Processional Way, Mshatta Palace facade

Details: World's largest collection of ancient architecture (currently limited access due to renovation)

Hours: Tue-Sun 10:00-18:00, Thu until 20:00

Neues Museum

€14 Book Recommended

Highlights: Nefertiti Bust, Egyptian collection, Prehistory artifacts

Details: Rebuilt after WWII, stunning architecture by David Chipperfield

Hours: Tue-Sun 10:00-18:00, Thu until 20:00

Bode Museum

€12 Optional Booking

Highlights: Byzantine Art, Sculpture collection, Numismatic collection

Details: Beautiful dome building at Museum Island's tip

Hours: Tue-Sun 10:00-18:00

Jewish Museum Berlin

Free Permanent €10 Temporary

Highlights: Daniel Libeskind architecture, German-Jewish history, Interactive exhibits

Details: Europe's largest Jewish museum with striking zigzag design

Hours: Daily 10:00-18:00

DDR Museum

€13.50 Booking Available

Highlights: Interactive GDR exhibits, Trabant car simulator, Authentic apartment

Details: Hands-on experience of life in East Germany

Hours: Daily 9:00-21:00

German Historical Museum

€7 No Booking Required

Highlights: 2000 years of German history, Weapons collection, Modern history

Details: From Roman times to present day

Hours: Daily 10:00-18:00

Natural History Museum

€11 Booking Available

Highlights: Dinosaur skeletons, Mineral collection, Interactive displays

Details: One of the world's largest natural history collections

Hours: Tue-Fri 9:30-18:00, Sat-Sun 10:00-18:00

Topography of Terror

Free No Booking Required

Highlights: Nazi history documentation, Gestapo headquarters site, Historical photos

Details: Located on former SS and Gestapo headquarters

Hours: Daily 10:00-20:00

Berlin Wall Memorial

Free No Booking Required

Highlights: Original wall sections, Documentation center, Escape stories

Details: 1.4km memorial preserving the Wall's history

Hours: Tue-Sun 10:00-18:00

Berlin Attractions

Must-see sights and unique experiences

Brandenburg Gate

Free No Booking Required

Highlights: Neoclassical architecture, Symbol of unity, Photo opportunities

Details: Berlin's most famous landmark and symbol of reunification

Hours: 24/7 accessible

Reichstag Dome

Free Registration Required

Highlights: 360° city views, Glass dome architecture, Parliamentary tours

Details: Book 2-3 weeks in advance, bring passport for security

Hours: Daily 8:00-21:30, last entry 21:00

TV Tower (Fernsehturm)

€27.50+ Book Recommended

Highlights: 368m viewing platform, Panoramic city views, VR experience available

Details: Berlin's tallest structure with restaurant option

Hours: Daily 9:00-24:00

Charlottenburg Palace

€19 Combined Book Recommended

Highlights: Baroque palace rooms, Rococo decoration, Palace gardens

Details: Berlin's largest palace complex with beautiful gardens

Hours: Tue-Sun 10:00-17:30 (summer), 10:00-16:30 (winter)

Berlin Zoo

€15 Online Tickets Available

Highlights: Panda bears, Aquarium, Historic zoo buildings

Details: One of the world's oldest zoos with over 20,000 animals

Hours: Daily 9:00-18:00 (varies by season)

East Side Gallery

Free No Booking Required

Highlights: Longest open-air gallery, Street art, Historical murals

Details: 1.3km section of Berlin Wall with artistic murals

Hours: 24/7 accessible
